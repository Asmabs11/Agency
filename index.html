Destinations

Bali

The Island of the Gods, better known as Bali, is an Indonesian island located between Java and Lombok. Separated from the latter two by the Bali Strait to the west and the Lombok Strait to the east, its total area is 5,637 km2. Bali is the most popular and well-known tourist destination in Indonesia.

Greece

The cube-shaped white houses of its two main towns, Fira and Oia, perch on cliffs overlooking an underwater caldera (crater). They overlook the sea, small islands to the west as well as beaches made up of black, red and white lava pebbles

Korea

It is known for its rolling, green countryside dotted with cherry trees and centuries-old Buddhist temples, as well as its coastal fishing villages, subtropical islands and high-tech cities like Seoul, its capital.
